Privacy & Security

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

1. Commitment to Data Privacy

At RePrompt, we believe that your creative assets and photographs belong to you. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection and privacy.

Unlike traditional image utilities that require you to upload your files to a remote database, RePrompt uses a modern, decentralized model where image analytics and forensics are processed locally on your device.

The Zero-Storage Guarantee

We never store, catalog, or inspect your uploaded files. All computer vision algorithms run via canvas-based metrics or local temporary buffers, which are instantly freed from memory once your forensic prompt report is generated.

2. Processing Architecture

Our application operates using two primary tiers of analysis:

  • Local Computer Vision (OpenCV & Canvas): Basic structural metadata such as contrast ratio, mean brightness, Sobel gradients (for shadows), and aspect ratio are calculated directly in-memory inside your browser or temporary server buffers.
  • Vision-Language Models (VLM): When querying Google Gemini for prompt anatomy reconstruction, only the image bytes and isolated metrics are transmitted via a secure, encrypted API call. This information is processed statelessly and is not used for model training.

3. Local Browser Storage

To facilitate a convenient user experience, your analysis history and performance scores (e.g., Daily Match streaks) are saved using your browser's localStorage.

This data remains sandboxed on your device and is never synced to external databases unless explicitly requested by you. You can clear this history at any time directly through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Integrations

This site uses the Google Gemini API to analyze images and reconstruct positive and negative prompts. This integration is bound by the Google APIs Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. No other tracking scripts, advertisement networks, or data-collection pixels are active.

5. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the security architecture of RePrompt, please visit our Contact Us page.

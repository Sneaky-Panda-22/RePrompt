About Us

RePrompt is an AI Vision Forensics & Prompt Engineering dashboard. It extracts physics data, lighting styles, color schemas, and camera angles from images — giving you the reverse-engineered prompt behind any AI-generated visual. Built for forensic analysts, prompt engineers, and curious creators who want to understand how an image was made.
